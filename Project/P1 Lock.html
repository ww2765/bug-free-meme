This rusty lock was discovered on the graden gate. Its metal surface is corroded and flaking, showing the slow work of rain, soil, and time. But it is still functional.

Why I Collected It

  • Its weathered texture tells a story of decay
  • Symbolizes secrecy and forgotten histories
  • Contrast between strength of iron and weakness from rust

Fun fact:Rust is actually iron oxide, which forms when iron reacts with oxygen and water. The process can even make a lock expand slightly.
