Smooth Stone

  • Back to Home
  • Next: Dried Leaf

This stone was found near the edge of the garden bed. Its surface is worn smooth, likely by years of rain and soil movement.

Why I Collected It

  • Feels grounding to hold
  • Natural patterning like a map
  • Represents time and erosion
"In every block of stone lies a statue..." – Michelangelo

Fun fact: stones like this are sometimes used as “worry stones.”
